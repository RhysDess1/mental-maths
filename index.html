How it works

You will get 20 questions in total. Each question shows on screen with a timer. You get 2 seconds to read, then the timer starts. Once time runs out, the next question appears automatically. You cannot go back to previous questions.

Easy (8s): multiplying and dividing by 10, 100, 1000; adding and subtracting hundreds and tens; number bonds; doubling and halving
Medium (10s): fractions and percentages of amounts, rounding, time conversions
Hard (15s): basic algebra, elapsed time, multi-step percentages

Tip: sit somewhere quiet, have a pencil and paper nearby for jotting (not for working out), and take the test in one go.
